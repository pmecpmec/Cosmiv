### Step 1: Choose Email Provider

**Recommended Options:**

#### Option A: Google Workspace (Recommended)
- **Cost:** ~$6/user/month (Business Starter) or $12/user/month (Business Standard)

- **Cons:** Higher cost for multiple users

- **Cost:** ~$6/user/month (Business Basic) or $12.50/user/month (Business Standard)
- **Pros:** Office suite included, good integration, professional
- **Cons:** Slightly more complex setup
- **Best for:** Teams that need Office suite

- **Cost:** ~$1/user/month (Mail Lite) or $4/user/month (Mail Premium)
- **Pros:** Affordable, good features
- **Cons:** Less brand recognition
- **Best for:** Cost-conscious setup

#### Option D: ProtonMail Business
- **Cost:** ~$7.99/user/month
- **Pros:** Privacy-focused, encrypted
- **Cons:** More expensive, less business features
- **Best for:** Privacy-first approach

**Recommendation:** Start with **Google Workspace Business Starter** ($6/user/month) for 2-3 users. Easy to scale later.

#### SPF Record (Sender Policy Framework)

**For Google Workspace:**
```
Type: TXT
Name: @
Value: v=spf1 include:_spf.google.com ~all
```

**For Microsoft 365:**
```
Type: TXT
Name: @
Value: v=spf1 include:spf.protection.outlook.com ~all
```

#### DKIM Record (DomainKeys Identified Mail)

1. **Generate DKIM Key in Admin Console:**

#### DMARC Record (Domain-based Message Authentication)

